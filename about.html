About Songsmania.com

Songsmania.com is brought to you by a team of dedicated & hard working professionals, the project decision had been confirmed at 2006 but we could not start it & thus it was postponed. It was then started at the ending months of 2007 & finally launched at 24-MAR-2008, we are really happy to get done on this project & present you Songsmania.com

Our Goal

Our goal since the very first day has been to develop a revolutionary infotainment site, the team tries its best to provide you the best infotainment content & we hope of completing our goal! We are yet in our beta version which means that the site is in its testing period and not the final completed version, we are checking user responses according to which Songsmania.com will be re-built, modified, expanded, polished & provide our visitors what they want!

How To Help Us? Since we are in our beta version we know that our visitors might face broken links, scripting errors, design mistakes & so on...

You can help us by:

  • Letting us know of any error that you might come across.
  • Sending us your feedback on the site, what you like & what you dont.
  • Sending your ideas for your own infotainment home 'Songsmania.Com'
